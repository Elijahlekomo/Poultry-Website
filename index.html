Who We Are

Leburu Poultry Farm offers a diverse range of high-quality services and services. Our offerings include premium quality eggs, day-one layer chicks, roosters, hens, and comprehensive training services tailored to aspiring farmers. Through our practical experience and commitment to excellence,
